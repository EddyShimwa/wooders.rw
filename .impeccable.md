## Design Context

### Users
- Primary users are non-technical customers in Rwanda shopping for handcrafted wood products for homes and businesses.
- Secondary users are business buyers (cafes, hotels, offices, event planners) who need branded or bulk wood items.
- Main job to be done: quickly understand products, trust quality, and place an order via WhatsApp with minimal friction.

### Brand Personality
- Warm, trustworthy, handcrafted.
- Local-first and practical.
- Premium but approachable.

### Aesthetic Direction
- Natural, refined, and clear.
- Emphasize craftsmanship without poetic or ambiguous wording.
- Keep copy plain-language, action-oriented, and confidence-building.

### Design Principles
- Clarity first: every label and CTA should be instantly understandable.
- Actionable text: always tell users what happens next.
- Consistent terminology: use the same words across pages (e.g., Collection, Reviews, Order on WhatsApp).
- Local relevance: prioritize Rwanda context and RWF currency formatting.
- Helpful tone: friendly, direct, and respectful in success/error states.
